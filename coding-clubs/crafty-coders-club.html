Four weeks of designing, making, and coding game controllers, digital instruments, robots and more using crafty materials.

Your children will learn the basics of electronics, computer programming, prototyping, and design while working on their own creations.

Every great product starts with a prototype - this course demonstrates how, with a little bit of knowledge, you can quickly test and prototype ideas that could change the future.

Learn about

  • Inputs and Outputs
  • Basic electronics
  • Product design and development
  • Block based programming
  • Problem solving
