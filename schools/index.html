After school CODING clubs with a focus on exploration, design, and creativity.


Rocket Coders work with schools across the UK to help turbo-boost their computing curriculum.

We work alongside computing departments to enrich the curriculum with hands on computing clubs that bring cutting edge technology into the classroom.

Rocket Coders believe that every child should have access to a world class digital education, and through our scholarship program we help to ensure no student is left behind in the digital revolution

Ada Lovelace Scholarships

For every club we provide we offer two free places for children who receive pupil premium or whos parents or carers receive income support.

Admin Free Clubs

Cutting Edge Ed-tech

We bring a wide variety of brand new educational technology into the classroom so you don't have to worry about investing in expensive equipment that quickly dates.

Strict Child-Protection Policy

We have a very strict child-protection policy and are covered by public liability insurance up to £2million. Our teachers are all friendly, engaging, and respectful of school rules, policies and traditions.

Bridgewater Primary School Berkhamsted

Rocket Coders are currently providing after-school coding clubs at Bridgewater Primary School in Berkhamsted, Hertfordshire for year 3 students. The students are making interactive games and applications in Scratch before moving on to 4 weks of robotics classes using the Crumble Micro-Controller.
