Bridgewater Primary School CODING club


Rocket Coders are working alongside the computing department at Bridgewater Primary School to provide students in year 3 with after school coding clubs

The coders are working on creating their own games, apps, and robotics projects while reinforcing their knowledge of sequence, selection, and repetition in line with the National Curriculum in Computing

Classes run every Tuesday and Thursday from 3.15 to 4.15 at the school.

Ada Lovelace Scholarship

Want a CODING club at your childs school?
