What are the class sizes of the clubs?

Class sizes for after-school coding clubs at our hack space are limited to six students per class. This ensures that every student gets the help they need regardless of ability. At school coding clubs we teach a maximum of 10 pupils per class to ensure that every student gets an opportuity to join the weekly clubs. To reflect the larger class sizes at schools prices are often reduced.

I can't afford the classes - is there help available?

Scholarships are available on all our school coding clubs and places on our weekly clubs are available at a discounted rate for parents who are on income support and who's children receive pupil premium payments at school. Please get in touch for more details.

Can my children keep what they make?

Anything your children 3D print or build they are welcome to take home - however some additional costs may apply for projects containing microcontrollers such as the MakeyMakey, Crumble, and Arduino. We will let you know before the club begins the additional cost to take projects home (if there is one).

Are my children assessed?

At Rocket Coders we don't believe that digital creativity can be measured through an examination - we look to see how coders use their imagination, logical and critical thinking skills to produce projects and takle problems. Following camps and clubs we'll give you a brief assesment form that will outline in what areas your child triumphed and where they may needa helping hand in the future.

Do I need to bring a laptop?

We ask that all children come with a working laptop for the classes with Google Chrome or FireFox installed. We are hoping to purchase laptops in the future but currently we only have a few available for hire. To check availability please contact the team before you attend your club.

Can my children bring snacks?

We provide healthy organic snacks at all our coding clubs but feel free to send your children with something extra. We ask that you don't send your children with anything containing nuts please.

Are you insured and DBS checked?

Yes we are fully insured and everyone in the building has a valid DBS certificate. All our staff are also qualified in first aid. For more information please see our child safeguarding policy here.

Can I see my childrens work?

We will upload anonymous projects to the Rocket Coders website and may show off student projects on our social media pages (with permission). For projects that can't be shared online we can email you images and videos at your request.

Can you start a club at my child's school?

We'd love to! Email us today to find out more about our parent ambassador program where you can receive free classes for your children in return for an introduction to their school.

More questions? Get in touch today info@rocketcoders.co.uk
